What is Box-Technic?

Box-Technic is a technical boxing class designed to teach and develop the fundamental skills of Boxing in a fun and safe environment. It will centre around Focus Mitt training with no sparring, that is, no punches to the head or body. Perfect for anyone looking to learn technical boxing skills, Box-Technic provides a fantastic workout without the stress of taking punches and getting injured.

Box-Technic is suitable for anyone aged 15+, regarless of fitness level. Since it will require you to work with a partner, Box-Technic is great for friends, families, and couples. That said, if you would like to register as an individual, we will arrange a partner for you.



( Age limit 15+ )

Box-Technic Level One
(Beginner)
10 Sessions

Box-Technic Level One is for anyone who wants to learn the foundational skills of boxing and focus mitt holding right from scratch. No prior boxing or padwork experience is required. The class will cover the following:

  • Boxing stance and guard position
  • Execution of the main punches: jab, cross, hooks, uppercuts
  • How to correctly and safely hold the focus mitts
  • Footwork technique
  • Defensive manoeuvres â€“ how to slip, roll and block punches
  • How to put punch combinations together

N.B: Due to the steep learning curve of this course, please ensure you can attend a minimum of 8 out of the 10 sessions.

Box-Technic Level Two
(Advanced)
10 Sessions

Box-Technic Level Two will build upon all of the skills developed in the Level 1 course and will allow you to:

  • Perform punch combinations right from the get go without having to learn all the punches again!
  • Learn new punch variations and perfect the basics
  • Perform longer, more advanced combinations
  • Learn more advanced defensive strategies and footwork
  • Push your conditioning to the limit!

Level Two is for those who have already completed the Box-Technic Level 1 class or have previous boxing/padwork experience. If you have previous experience but are unsure on whether the Level Two is suitable for you, please send an email explaining your previous experience so we can advise you.

Do I need my own gloves & handwraps?

If you have your own boxing gloves and focus mitts we encourage you to bring these with you. However, boxing gloves and focus mitts will be provided for each session if you do not already own them. Buying your own pair of handwraps and learning how to wrap your hands is strongly advised, as the class will not cover this! You can buy handwraps from most of the main sports retailers and there are some good sources on the Internet to teach you how to wrap your hands.
